Ancient caloric functions and parabolic frequency on graphs

Archana Mohandas and Tang-Kai Lee

Proceedings of the American Mathematical Society, 2025

Abstract: We establish sharp bounds for spaces of ancient solutions to discrete heat equations on various weighted graphs. Our methods introduce new techniques in discrete geometric analysis, utilizing parabolic frequency functions to control the growth and decay of solutions. These results have applications to understanding heat flow on networks and provide discrete analogs of classical results in geometric analysis.
